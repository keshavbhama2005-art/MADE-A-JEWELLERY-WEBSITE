Our Story

Four decades of golden craftsmanship

Founded in 1985 in the heart of Gurugram, Krishna Jewellers was born from a single goldsmith's dream — to bring the finest Indian jewellery craftsmanship to every family's most precious moments.

Today, spanning three generations, we remain committed to purity, artistry, and the timeless beauty that gold represents in every Indian home.
